What is a personal loan?

A personal loan is an unsecured loan that can be used for various personal expenses such as travel, weddings, medical expenses, higher education, and home renovations.

What are the uses of personal loan?

A personal loan can be used for almost any personal expense, such as:

  • Personal loan for higher education

  • Personal loan for a medical emergency

  • Personal loan for a wedding

  • Personal loan for home expenses

  • Personal loan for travel

You can use our Personal Loan EMI Calculator to find out the total interest payable for your personal loan. It depends on the interest rate, loan amount, and your repayment duration.

  • Loan amount ranging from Rs. 40000 to Rs. 55 lakh.

How to choose the best online personal loan in India?

To choose the best online personal loan in India, compare lenders on factors such as interest rates, processing fees, eligibility and repayment tenures first. Use an EMI calculator to estimate your monthly EMIs.

How does personal loan balance transfer work?

A personal loan balance transfer works by moving your existing loan to another lender providing better terms. The new lender will pay out your outstanding amount, and you can continue repayment with them according to their terms and conditions.

How does a personal loan work?

When you take a personal loan, lender will give you a lump-sum amount that can be used to manage various personal expenses. You then repay it through fixed EMIs.

How long does it take to get personal loan approved?

With proper eligibility, a personal loan is approved within 5 minutes.
